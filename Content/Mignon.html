First Group Project: Jigsawville

Info

  • Made with a team of 10students.
  • Software Used: Photoshop, Subtance Designer, After Effects.

I was in charge of 2D assets including the animated portal effect.

First year group work

In a group of 10 people with 1Planner, 7programmers and 2designers inlcuding me we made this game as an end of first year project. We all brainstormed on what kind of game to do, the theme was "change" and we came up with this tile system you can rotate to form a path to the exit. The first prototype had a much more happy vibe with more colors but in the end we though the Noire style was more appealing. I used After Effect to make the animated portal and Substance Designer to make the tileble stone bricks.
Working in a group for the first time, even with only 9other students, taught me a lot about deadlines, communication and group work. We constantly had to talk to each other, especially me with the other designer and with the Planner, we would divide the work between designer and submit proposal to the planner, the planner would also come to us with specific requests to finish by a deadline.
